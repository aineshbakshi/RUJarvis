Who Are We

Have you ever used the Rutgers nextbus app? Ever wished that the app told you something better than the number of minutes before
the next bus arrives at every bus stop?? Ever wished that you could just enter your destination in an app and have it tell you
exactly which bus-stop to walk to and which bus to take to reach your destination at the quickest pace? Well, if you did,
then meet us! We are a group of nerdy kids trying to improve the current app by creating an interactive one that can
take in crazy inputs like "I want to reach the electrical engineering building from hickman hall" and
output the most optimized path users can take, thus, saving all our precious time.
